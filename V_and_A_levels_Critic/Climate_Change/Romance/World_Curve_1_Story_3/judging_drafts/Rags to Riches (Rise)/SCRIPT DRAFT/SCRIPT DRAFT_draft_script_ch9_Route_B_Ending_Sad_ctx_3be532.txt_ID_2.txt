Mayor Lionel Park: (a brief, ragged laugh) "If only memories kept the lights on."

Captain Reyes stops too close to Lionel, eyes a map of weathered truth. He grips the gatepost with hands that have hauled nets and hauled sorrow. "This town has a memory like the sea. It'll pull you under if you ignore it. I will not stand aside if you drown us for a promise."

The mayor's face folds into the shape of someone trying to hold two oceans apart. No answer comes that satisfies both.
