[Scene: Promenade Pilot Site | Midday]

[Music: Percussive rhythm building]

Celeste's temporary office trailer sits up on a low rise, a silver lapel pin glinting as she steps out with people who look like they could write checks in their sleep. Elias: "We can structure the pilot to hire locally for labor and monitoring. Amina and I can write stipulations — short-term work for community members, apprenticeship for monitoring. No one gets left out of payroll."
