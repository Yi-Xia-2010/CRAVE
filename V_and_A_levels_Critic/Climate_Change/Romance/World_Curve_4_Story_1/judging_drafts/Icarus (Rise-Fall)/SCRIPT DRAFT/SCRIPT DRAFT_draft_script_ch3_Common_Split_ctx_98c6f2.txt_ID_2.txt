Celeste Park: "Our proposal stabilizes the shoreline, brings jobs, and invites investment without sacrificing Solhaven's character."

Her voice is calm, but you feel the edges of it — promise wrapped in presentation. A few people near the front nod; behind them, others shift uneasily. The render's gloss doesn't contain the smell of wet soil, or the small terraces where you and Rae transplanted halophytes last year.

Mara Lin: "We need to see the ground, not just the render. A pilot that puts living systems first — and keeps redevelopment reversible — is the bridge we should test."
