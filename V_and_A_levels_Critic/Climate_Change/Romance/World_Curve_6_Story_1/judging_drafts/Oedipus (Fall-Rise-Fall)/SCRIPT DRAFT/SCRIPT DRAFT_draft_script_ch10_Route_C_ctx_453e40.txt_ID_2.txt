You feel the tilt of possibility. Co-ownership means data that the neighborhood can use — photos, salinity trends, early warnings. Mirrored readouts mean transparency. Third-party audits mean someone other than the firm can call out malfeasance. The fail-safe clause is ugly, but perhaps manageable if written tightly.
