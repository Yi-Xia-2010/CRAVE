Investor Lead: (smooth) "We can build governance structures. We prefer a centralized manager for operational efficiency. It keeps liability clear."

Elias shifts. You can see the movement in the small details: the amber in his eyes shutters, as if someone has drawn a blind across a window. His fingers brush the leather of his camera — a nervous, almost absent gesture that says he is holding on to something familiar while the ground changes.

Your pulse draws tightly. Around the table, the lab's instruments hum like a chorus holding its breath.
