Mateo: "And the ordinance's phased structure? We knot the legal language to pilot outcomes and a public accountability mechanism. That gives neighborhoods time to adapt and forces corporations to meet ecological standards before drawing down funds."

Mara Solenne [steady]: "Then let's write the signatures into this. Not only commitments, but timelines. We make failure expensive and success inevitable."

Lucia Montrose [a touch of warmth]: "You and your metaphors, Mara. Fine. Make success inevitable. Sign here."

Mateo hands you a pen. Your hand trembles a fraction, not with fear but with the weight of the people who will depend on this. You sign.
