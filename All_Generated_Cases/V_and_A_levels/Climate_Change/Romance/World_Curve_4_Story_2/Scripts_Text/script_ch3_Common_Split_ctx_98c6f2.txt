[Dialogue: The exchange tightens; multiple turns follow, voices overlapping with intent.]

Maya Soren: "We embed veto points. We hold funds in escrow. We require neighborhood-led placement decisions for any physical works in Lower Quay."

Grantley [leaning forward]: "Escrow is workable. But every delay costs lives and property. A fast track allows visible protections sooner. Think of the houses that will be spared this season."
