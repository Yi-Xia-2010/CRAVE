Asha Moreno [Scornful]: "Buy time for what? For lawyers to rearrange terms? For developers to stage relief concerts and call it 'community building'?"

Lila Park [Cold]: "Asha. This is getting personal. We are dealing with existential risks. We cannot wait indefinitely for pilot projects while houses fall and people drown. We have a model that works. We have partners ready to scale this. If Marabel wants to be a model, we can save thousands—"

Asha Moreno [Stepping forward, close enough to see the tiny camera at the edge of Lila's cuff]: "Not if saving thousands means stealing the shore from the few hundred who built it. You do not 'save' a town by auctioning its memory."
