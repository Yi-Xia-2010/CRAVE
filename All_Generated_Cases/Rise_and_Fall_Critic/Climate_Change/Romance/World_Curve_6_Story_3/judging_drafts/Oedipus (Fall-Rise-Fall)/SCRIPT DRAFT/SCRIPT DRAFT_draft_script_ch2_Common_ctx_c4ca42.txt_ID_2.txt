You: "I was at the kelp line. We found an old sign—reminded me why we do this." (You close your notebook a little harder than necessary.) "Show me."

Priya: "This is what Cass's aide wanted. Short-term protective effect by neighborhood, cost, time-to-deploy, equity modifiers.
