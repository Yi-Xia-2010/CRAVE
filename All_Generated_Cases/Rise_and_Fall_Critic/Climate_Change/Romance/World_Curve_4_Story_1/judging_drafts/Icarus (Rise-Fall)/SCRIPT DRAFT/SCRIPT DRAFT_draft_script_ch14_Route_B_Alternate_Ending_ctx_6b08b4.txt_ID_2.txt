Old Man Toma: (hoarse) "They made it bright, Maia. Bright enough to see everything that's been swept under."

Old Man Toma: (looking out to the leveled docks) "Saved what lives? The lives that had contracts? The lives that fit into ledgers? I know the ledgers; I used to read them when I was young. There is room in them for numbers. There is less room for song."
