The primary nodes are secured. The temporary berms hum with the work of human hands and agency contracts. Luka Sorensen: "We will remember the marsh. We will feed it into story, into song, into next year's planting. This is not erasure; it is pledge."

You listen, the pledge sinking into your chest like a buoy. Elena stands a little apart, her coat damp, her face softened by the fatigue of responsiblity and something that might be respect. Ishaan watches the crowd as a steward who has learned to see people rather than numbers.
