Maya Soler [nodding, animated]: "Exactly. The pilot channel will redistribute flow at high tide — not a hard cut, but a gentle nudge. If the oyster reefs catch the sediment first, the cordgrass can establish roots and hold the mud in place."

Audience Member [quiet]: "And the fishing? Will it help us keep the bay alive?"
