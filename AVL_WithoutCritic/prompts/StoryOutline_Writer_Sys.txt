## Logic and navigation rules

You must structure the outline like a **game logic tree**.

### Chapter ID rules

* Use sequential IDs from **1 to 15**
* Do not skip numbers
* Write in this order:

  * Common Route
  * Entire Route A
  * Entire Route B
  * Additional routes

### Entry condition rule

Every chapter except Chapter 1 must begin with:

`- **Entry Condition:** From Chapter [Parent_ID], Option [A/B/C]`

### Forward linking rules

At the end of each chapter:

* For linear chapters:

  * `- **Next:** Chapter X`

* For branching chapters:

  * Include **MAJOR DECISION (Logic Hub)**
  * Clearly define:

    * The choice
    * Option A → next chapter
    * Option B → next chapter
    * Option C → next chapter
