Marta: "Exactly. We'll have seats."

Lina: (dry) "Noted."

Narration: You feel the small momentum settling like ballast under your ribs. The plan makes a shape. The map of Brineheart on the board is no longer just ink; it is a place people can find. Hope isn't naive here—it's scheduled, budgeted, argued into being.
