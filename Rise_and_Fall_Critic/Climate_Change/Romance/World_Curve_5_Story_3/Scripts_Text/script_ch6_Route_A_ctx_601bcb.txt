Mateo watches you, the expression in his eyes not an engineer's calculation but something closer to a barometer—sensitive to pressure. There is a risk in saying no: funds sacrificed, opportunities foregone, another city rising faster because it took the money and buried local voices. Mateo Hale [softening]: "You did the hard thing. And you convinced enough people to walk it with you."
