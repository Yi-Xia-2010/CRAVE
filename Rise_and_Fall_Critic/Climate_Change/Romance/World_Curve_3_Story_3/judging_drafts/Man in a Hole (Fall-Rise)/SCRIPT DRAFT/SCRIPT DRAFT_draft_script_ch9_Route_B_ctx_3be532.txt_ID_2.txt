The tradeoff still tastes like sea-salt and something sharper.

You inhale, and the air tastes like work: wet timber, diesel, rosemary from a passerby's tote. There is, impossibly, a kind of calm under the urgency — a rhythm to the repairs. Not victory. Repair.

The oversight committee — equal parts neighborhood reps and technical advisors — convenes for its first public meeting in the community hall. The room hums with careful civility.
